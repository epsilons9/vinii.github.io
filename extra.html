about lilly

30+, she/her, and Canadian. Hanging out in fandom spaces since the early 2000s. I've been writing stories since I was a kid and have been publishing my fanfics online since 2002. I went to university to specialize in Nordic and Baltic history and still enjoy doing research in this area, especially in Estonian history. My other interests include genealogy, gaming, music, astrology, computers, and abandoned places.
